How to solve Uknown Server Error?

1. Go to search in windows and search view network connections
2. Right click your network connection and select properties
3. In that scroll down until you see internet protocol version 4 and version 6, disable 6 and double click internet protocol ver 4 (do not disable it your internet willl be lost).
4. Click on the option "use the following DNS server addresses" and type in that
Preffered DNS Server: 1.1.1.1
Alternate DNS Server: 1.0.0.1
5. Then click ok and restart your pc
